Create a world-class luxury travel website that feels like an interactive digital experience rather than a traditional travel agency website. The design must feel premium, cinematic, immersive, and emotionally engaging. Avoid the typical AI-generated SaaS appearance, neon gradients, excessive cards, dashboard layouts, boxed sections, or generic templates. Every section should flow naturally into the next through motion, depth, storytelling, and visual transitions.

The website should be designed around the concept of "Journey Through the World" where the user feels like they are traveling while scrolling.

VISUAL DIRECTION

Use a sophisticated luxury travel aesthetic with deep ocean blues, warm sunset tones, snowy mountain whites, tropical turquoise waters, rich earth colors, and cinematic atmospheric lighting.

The design should feel inspired by:

* Luxury travel magazines
* National Geographic cinematography
* High-end tourism campaigns
* Modern interactive storytelling websites
* Premium resort and adventure brands

Avoid:

* Neon colors
* Pink/purple AI gradients
* Generic glass cards everywhere
* Excessive borders
* Overuse of buttons
* Corporate SaaS layouts

The interface should feel fluid, organic, and spacious with large breathing room between content.

HERO EXPERIENCE

The homepage begins with a full-screen cinematic scene.

Instead of a static hero image, create a massive 3D environment featuring:

* Mountain ranges
* Clouds moving through valleys
* Flying birds
* Distant waterfalls
* Dynamic sunlight
* Atmospheric fog

The user initially sees only a breathtaking world landscape.

As the user moves their cursor:

* Depth layers react subtly
* Foreground and background move independently
* Clouds shift naturally
* Light rays move slightly

A large bold statement emerges naturally from the environment:

"THE WORLD IS WAITING"

The typography should feel monumental, elegant, and integrated into the scenery rather than sitting on top of it.

Scrolling should feel like entering the landscape.

SCROLL EXPERIENCE

The entire website should function as a continuous storytelling journey.

Every scroll movement should feel smooth and cinematic.

Avoid traditional section jumps.

Instead:

The camera appears to move through different travel environments:

1. Mountains
2. Tropical Islands
3. Desert Landscapes
4. Historic Cities
5. Luxury Resorts
6. Adventure Destinations

Each environment transitions naturally into the next.

The user should feel like they are flying across the world.

3D WORLD ELEMENT

A highly detailed floating globe appears during scrolling.

The globe should:

* Rotate slowly
* React to cursor movement
* Shift position throughout the page
* Reveal different continents
* Display animated travel routes
* Show destination markers

The globe should never feel static.

It acts as the storytelling centerpiece of the website.

PARALLAX DEPTH SYSTEM

Create multiple depth layers:

Foreground:

* Plants
* Rocks
* Sand
* Water splashes
* Clouds

Midground:

* Travelers
* Buildings
* Vehicles
* Boats

Background:

* Mountains
* Oceans
* Cities
* Sky

Each layer moves at different speeds while scrolling.

This should create an authentic sense of depth and exploration.

DESTINATIONS EXPERIENCE

Instead of standard cards, destinations emerge from the environment.

As the user scrolls:

Massive destination showcases appear.

Each destination should occupy most of the screen.

Examples:

Swiss Alps
Maldives
Santorini
Dubai
Kyoto
Bali
Iceland

Each destination includes:

* Cinematic imagery
* Floating location details
* Smooth entrance animations
* Travel highlights
* Dynamic statistics
* Hover interactions

Content should feel embedded into the scene rather than placed inside boxes.

DISCOVERY SECTION

Create a "Discover Extraordinary Places" experience.

As the user scrolls horizontally through this section:

Large destinations slide into view.

Images should overlap elegantly.

Motion should feel premium and weighty.

The transitions should resemble a luxury travel documentary.

ADVENTURE EXPERIENCE

Introduce immersive 3D travel elements:

* Hot air balloons
* Airplanes
* Sailboats
* Helicopters
* Wildlife
* Mountain climbers

These elements should move subtly through the page.

Their motion should create life without becoming distracting.

TRAVEL TIMELINE

Create a visual travel route experience.

The user follows a journey path through the page.

The route animates while scrolling.

Pins appear on the path showing:

* Destinations
* Experiences
* Popular routes
* Hidden gems

This section should feel like following a real expedition map.

LUXURY EXPERIENCE SECTION

Introduce premium travel experiences.

The environment transforms into:

* Luxury resorts
* Infinity pools
* Ocean villas
* Mountain lodges
* Private islands

Use elegant motion and cinematic transitions.

Everything should feel aspirational.

TESTIMONIAL EXPERIENCE

Instead of testimonial cards:

Create floating traveler stories.

Their stories appear naturally while scrolling.

Portraits subtly animate.

Background scenes continue moving behind them.

The experience should feel human and emotional.

FINAL CTA EXPERIENCE

The final section should be unforgettable.

The user arrives at a stunning world view from space.

The entire globe becomes visible.

Travel routes illuminate across continents.

Stars slowly move.

The message appears:

"YOUR NEXT ADVENTURE BEGINS HERE"

Below it:

A single premium booking call-to-action.

The ending should feel emotional, inspiring, and memorable.

MOTION SYSTEM

All animations must feel:

* Smooth
* Natural
* Premium
* Weight-based
* Cinematic

Avoid:

* Bouncy animations
* Cheap fades
* Generic slide-ups
* Repetitive effects

Every movement should have purpose.

OVERALL FEELING

The website should feel like a luxury interactive travel documentary, combining cinematic storytelling, immersive 3D environments, scroll-driven experiences, depth, exploration, and emotion.

Visitors should leave feeling inspired to travel before they ever click a booking button.
